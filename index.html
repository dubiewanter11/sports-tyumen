About the Project

We are first-year students. We know that gym memberships are expensive. We created this map to help students find free sports facilities in Tyumen. Just click on the marker and find out where to go.

Why We Created This

We conducted a survey. 80% of students said they didn't know about free sports locations in the city. So we decided to help.

Our goal is simple: sport is for everyone
